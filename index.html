Frequently Asked Questions

Is FUE hair transplant painful?
Local anesthesia ensures absolutely zero pain during the procedure. Most patients describe it as comfortable and even fall asleep during the session.
When will I see final results?
Initial growth at 4 months, significant density at 8 months, and final full results at 12-14 months post-procedure.
How long is recovery time?
Return to work in 3-5 days, scabs fall off by day 10, normal activities resume within 2 weeks.
Are results permanent?
Yes! Transplanted hair is genetically resistant to DHT and will last a lifetime with proper care.
